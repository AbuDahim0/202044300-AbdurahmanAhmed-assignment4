## 🚫 Mistakes to Avoid

- ❌ Reading too fast — aim for ~140 words per minute.
- ❌ Forgetting to demo the GitHub API section.
- ❌ Forgetting to mention how AI was used responsibly.
- ❌ Calling the US market tools "financial advice" or "recommendations" — they are software and data exploration tools.
- ❌ Revealing private client names or confidential project details.
- ❌ Claiming formal employment or internships — describe everything as project-based, freelance, or client-based.
- ❌ Submitting the script file instead of the actual recorded video.
- ❌ Recording with a broken page (refresh once before starting).
- ❌ Going over 7 minutes — record a second take if needed.
- ❌ Recording with audio notifications enabled.

---

## 🎥 Recording Settings

- **Resolution:** 1080p (1920×1080) minimum.
- **Frame rate:** 30 fps is enough.
- **Audio:** mono is fine; ensure no background hum.
- **Cursor:** make it visible (most screen recorders have a setting).
- **Click highlight:** enable if your tool supports it — it makes demos easier to follow.

Recommended free tools: macOS *Screen Recorder* (Cmd+Shift+5), OBS Studio, Loom, QuickTime.
